Cappadocia

Visit Cappadocia!

Located in Central Anatolia, Turkey, Cappadocia feels like a place that is simply put out of this world.

It is composed of many rock formations that are called “fairy chimneys” and volcanic peaks which will make you feel like you are dreaming, almost as if you have steeped foot in Mars.

Cappadocia has a fascinating history; it was once belonging to the Hittite Empire which is one of the first empires in the world. It later was home to Persia, the Roman Empire, the Byzantine Empire, Turkish clans, the Ottoman Empire and lastly, Turkey. With this rich history you can see many old churches, carvings and even cave houses that existed in the past.

Not only is Cappadocia great for history lovers; it's also a great place to visit for nature lovers and adventure lovers alike with it's surreal geography and many fun activities that the place offers

Things To Do In Cappadocia

1. Stay in A Cave Hotel: A trip to Capaddocia is most memorable when you stay in a cave hotel. Here is a list of cave hotels. Staying at a cave hotel will make you feel like you have been transported back in time and will make you have a feel of what life was for people who lived in Cappadocia in the past.

2. Göreme National Museum: A spectacular landscape that is entirely sculpted by erosion, the Göreme Valley is protected by UNESCO World Heritage Sites. Here you can see many sculptures, art pieces and remainders of human life dating back to the 4th century.

3. Hot Air Balloon Ride: Fly above the spectacular rock formations to have a truly remarkable experience that you will for sure never forget.

4. Attend The Cappadox Festival: Cappadox is an annual festival that happens in Cappadocia. I attended this festival in 2018 and it was truly an experience that I will never forget. The festival is filled with music concerts, picnics, bike rides, art exhibitions and more. The festival will truly make you appreciate this magical land.
