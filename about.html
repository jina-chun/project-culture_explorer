About Us

Bio

We are three university students from Korea, Russia and Turkey who share a common passion for travel.

Aylin: I was born in Istanbul, Turkey; a city that has cultural influences both from the East and the West which has always made me interested in exploring different cultures and understanding how history has effected the way things are today. I really love to wander and explore; when I am travelling I really like to get a feel of what life is like for the people that live in the place that I am travelling to ; so I like to take the approach of "living like a local" in the places that I travel to. I also really enjoy cultural sites.

Jina: I was born in Tokyo, Japan, and lived in 4 other countries: Japan, Singapore, Thailand, New Zealand, and before I came to Boston. Exploring and living in different countries has inspired me to experience and learn about various cultures. My passion is not only to travel but to understand, appreciate and acquire knowledge in diverse cultures. I love to travel and be part of the local environment. The destinations of my journeys are centered around food, hence my travel plan often focuses on the must-go-to restaurants in the area. I wish to share the best places I have been to!
