Travel Guide to Singapore

Yong Jin Chun Apr 21, 2021 0 Comments

Singapore (official name: Republic of Singapore) is a Sovereign island, city-state in Southeast Asia.
The population of Singapore is about 5.7 million residents, it has the second greatest population destiny in the world.

Interesting fact: There are four official languages of Singapore: English, Malay, Chinese and Tamil.
